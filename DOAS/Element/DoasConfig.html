STARTUP & SAFETY

Power On: 24VDC to MegaBas HAT → Initialize I/O to safe state → All outputs DE-ENERGIZED

Safety Checks: Supply air temperature sensor operational → Occupancy schedule check → Freeze protection monitoring → Temperature limits monitoring

Ready State: Awaiting occupancy period → All interlocks satisfied

NORMAL OPERATION - HEATING MODE (OAT < 65°F)

When outdoor air temperature drops below 65°F and space is occupied:

Condition Action
Supply Temp < Setpoint Enable supply fan and OA damper
Heating Required Enable heat (Triac 2) and modulate gas valve (AO2)
Temperature Error Calculation Gas valve position = (Setpoint - Supply Temp) × 10%, minimum 26%
At/Above Setpoint Close gas valve (0%), maintain fan operation

NORMAL OPERATION - COOLING MODE (OAT ≥ 65.5°F)

2-stage DX cooling based on supply air temperature:

Stage Condition Action
Stage 1 Supply Temp > Setpoint + 2°F Enable Stage 1 chiller (Triac 3)
Stage 2 Supply Temp > Setpoint + 4°F Enable Stage 1 + Stage 2 chillers (Triac 3 & 4)
Satisfied Supply Temp ≤ Setpoint + 2°F Disable cooling stages, maintain fan operation

DEHUMIDIFICATION MODE

Entry Conditions: OAT > 55°F AND (Outdoor RH > 85% OR Indoor RH > 55%)

Exit Conditions: OAT ≤ 55°F OR (Outdoor RH < 83% AND Indoor RH < 53%)

Operation: Enable Stage 1 cooling • Test cooling effectiveness (1 min) • If proven effective (5°F drop), add modulating heat • Maintain simultaneous cooling and heating for dehumidification

Safety: Space temperature monitoring prevents overheating/overcooling • Heating only enabled after cooling proven effective

FREEZE PROTECTION

Activation: Supply air temp < 40°F for 5 minutes during occupied hours

Action: Close OA damper (Triac 1 OFF) • Maintain fan operation • Enable heating if available

Recovery: When supply air temp ≥ 40°F, reopen OA damper after 5-minute timer reset

OCCUPANCY CONTROL

Occupied Hours: 07:00-21:45 daily

Occupancy Bypass: When enabled, unit operates as always occupied

Unoccupied Mode: All equipment OFF • OA damper CLOSED • Energy saving mode

ENABLE CONDITIONS & SHUTDOWN

Enable Requires: Occupied period (or bypass active) • Supply temp sensor valid • No critical temperature limits exceeded • Freeze protection not active (or timed reset)

Normal Shutdown: Unoccupied period • Close OA damper • Disable all heating/cooling • Stop supply fan

Emergency Shutdown: High/low temperature limits exceeded • Immediate shutdown of all equipment • Close OA damper • Activate alarms

SAFETY LIMITS

Parameter Limit Action
High Supply Air Temp > 85°F Emergency shutdown all equipment
Low Supply Air Temp < 45°F Emergency shutdown all equipment
Heating Lockout OAT > 70°F Disable heating system
Cooling Lockout OAT < 50°F Disable cooling system
